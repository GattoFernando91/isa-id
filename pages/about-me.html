Hello!

How are you?

I'll tell you a little about myself. My name is Isabelle Mallwitz and I am 24 years old and currently living in Berlin, Germany. From 2015 to 2020 I lived in Buenos Aires, Argentina, where I studied Interior Design at UADE.

I speak three languages; German, Spanish and English.

I consider myself a curious person and open for new experiences. I always try to challenge myself and expand my comfort zone, because for me, it's not about doing things that make you uncomfortable, but starting to feel comfortable with new activities or techniques.

I apply this philosophy to my personal life, as well as my professional life as a designer. Little by little I am expanding my knowledge to integrate it into my work.
