One drop can make a rainbow

OFF-WHITE x Evian

In the unexpected collaboration of OFF-WHITE and Evian, designer Virgil Abloh wanted to show that within something as pure, clean and transparent as water, a rainbow can be found in each drop when illuminated by light. The designer uses this situation as a metaphor for the infinite number of possibilities for every person.

Contrast

To incorporate both brands in the design of the booth, Evian and OFF-WHITE, Evian was represented by a clean and pure concept in the majority of the stand, being the main brand of this collab; however, for the sculptural centerpiece, inspiration was drawn from the collaboration's color palette and the designer Virgil Abloh. In addition, reference was made to the phrase "Rainbow inside", hinting that visitors are able to find a literal rainbow in the center of the booth.

Color Pallete

Exterior

AThe exterior structure of the booth is made up four main walls, referencing both brands. On two of these walls the main slogan of the collaboration can be found ("One drop can make a rainbow"), while on the remaining two walls the iconic Evian mountains can be found.
The colourful strips representing the waterdrop forming a rainbow can be seen from the exterior to cath the attention of the visitors.
